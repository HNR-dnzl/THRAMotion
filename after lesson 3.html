When a force involves direct contact between two bodies, such as a push or pull that you exert on an object with your hand, we call it acontact force. In addition to contact forces, there arelong range forcesthat act even when the bodies are separated by empty space. The force between two magnets is an example of a long-range force, as is the force of gravity.
